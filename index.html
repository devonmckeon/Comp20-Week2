ClinicCar

Catch a safe, free ride to your reproductive health clinic!

Reproductive health care is a necessary but often inaccessible human right. Unfortunately, certain groups have found ways to stand in the way of individuals and their personal business at reproductive health clinics. At ClinicCar, we know how inconvenient and intimidating visiting the clinic can be. That’s why we’re here to provide you with safe transportation to and from your appointment!
